ABOUT US

Sisa Crystal Mosaics are colourful bright Crystal glass mosaic. If you are looking for a real shine and brilliance Sisa crystal mosaic is the ideal product for it. We have wide range of colours in different sizes like 25x25 mm & 100x100 mm. Thickness of 8 mm gives more depth, enhance colour vibrancy & strength. It is available in various finishes like Gloss, Matte, Ice Broke. You can mix & merge different finishes like gloss, matte & ice broke to get an unique look.

You can create thousands of ideas & themes for bathrooms, swimming pools, kitchen backsplashes with beautiful colours like aquamarine blue to vibrant yellow.

We, at Sisa, assist our clients in giving unique identity to each & every project. We have a dedicated creative department which takes care of all creative needs. All you need to do is to imagine the creative possibilities with the Sisa crystal mosaic.
